Dawn Song's Bio

Professor Song is a Professor in Computer Science at UC Berkeley and Director of Berkeley Center on Responsible Decentralized Intelligence. She works in AI, AI safety & security, and decentralization. Her work has won numerous awards, including MacArthur Fellowship, Guggenheim Fellowship, more than 10 Test-of-Time Awards and Best Paper Awards at top Machine Learning and Computer Security conferences, She is ranked the most cited scholar in computer security. She is also a serial entrepreneur, having founded multiple successful startups, named on the Female Founder 100 List by Inc. and Wired25 List of Innovators.
